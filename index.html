What you can do

Keep, repair, recycle.

  • 🔧Repair before replacing — every extra year cuts new-mine pressure.
  • ♻️Recycle the old device responsibly — cobalt can re-enter the supply chain.
  • 📣Ask brands for traceable, audited cobalt — demand creates the pressure.
